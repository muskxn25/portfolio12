About Me

Hello myself Muskan, currently pursuing B.Tech from DEPSTAR, CHARUSAT in Information Technology Department. Good design isn't about fancy gadgets, bleeding-edge technology or trendy fads. It's about communication and building brand integrity. Gadgets, technology and fads aren't bad things when used correctly, but when misused they can clutter the design instead of reinforcing your message.

I believe that good designs creates conections. These connections are developed by communicating your story in a relevant, accessible way. Your logo, business card and website say something about yourbrand and tell your customers something about you.
